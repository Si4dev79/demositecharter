FISH SPECIES

Discover the exciting game fish you can catch in Aruba's waters

Blue Marlin

The ultimate trophy fish, known for spectacular jumps and fighting ability. Can reach over 1,000 pounds.

Best Season: May - October

Mahi-Mahi

Also known as Dorado, these colorful fish are known for their acrobatic fights and delicious meat.

Best Season: Year-round

Wahoo

One of the fastest fish in the ocean, known for blistering runs and excellent table fare.

Best Season: November - April

Yellowfin Tuna

Powerful fighters that can test any angler's endurance. Prized for sushi-grade meat.

Best Season: March - August

Sailfish

Known for their distinctive sail-like dorsal fin and incredible speed. A beautiful gamefish.

Best Season: January - April

Barracuda

Aggressive predators with razor-sharp teeth. Known for explosive strikes and aerial displays.

Best Season: Year-round
