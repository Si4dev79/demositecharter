FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find answers to common questions about our fishing charters in Aruba. If you don't see your question here, feel free to contact us!

Do I need fishing experience to join a charter?

No experience is necessary! Our experienced crew will provide all the guidance you need. We welcome anglers of all skill levels, from complete beginners to seasoned pros. Our crew will help you with everything from baiting hooks to landing your catch.

What's included in the charter price?

Our charter prices include all fishing equipment (rods, reels, tackle, bait), fishing licenses, bottled water and soft drinks. Full-day charters also include lunch. You're welcome to bring your own food and beverages as well.

What fish can we expect to catch?

Aruba's waters are home to a variety of game fish including Blue Marlin, White Marlin, Sailfish, Mahi-Mahi (Dorado), Wahoo, Yellowfin Tuna, Blackfin Tuna, Barracuda, and various species of Snapper. The specific fish available vary by season, but there's always something exciting to catch!

Can we keep the fish we catch?

Yes, you can keep most fish you catch for consumption. We practice catch and release for billfish (Marlin and Sailfish) to help preserve these magnificent species. For other fish like Mahi-Mahi, Wahoo, and Tuna, you're welcome to keep them. We can clean and fillet your catch for you to take back to your accommodation or to a local restaurant that will cook it for you.

What should I bring on the fishing trip?

We recommend bringing:

  • Sunscreen (SPF 30+)
  • Sunglasses (polarized are best)
  • Hat or cap
  • Light, breathable clothing
  • Camera
  • Motion sickness medication if you're prone to seasickness
  • Cash for gratuities
  • Valid ID

How many people can join a charter?

Our boat can comfortably accommodate up to 8 passengers. The base price includes up to 4 people, with an additional fee per person beyond that. For the most comfortable fishing experience, we recommend 4-6 anglers.

What if the weather is bad?

Your safety is our top priority. If weather conditions are unsafe for fishing, we'll contact you to reschedule your trip or provide a full refund. Aruba generally has excellent weather year-round, so cancellations are rare.

Do you provide transportation to and from the boat?

Transportation is not included in the charter price. Our boat is docked at Oranjestad Harbor, which is easily accessible by taxi from most hotels and resorts. If you need assistance with transportation arrangements, please let us know when booking.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance, especially during peak tourist season (December to April). However, we'll always try to accommodate last-minute bookings when possible.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require a 50% deposit to secure your booking. Cancellations made more than 7 days before your scheduled trip receive a full refund. Cancellations within 7 days of your trip are subject to a 50% cancellation fee unless we can rebook your slot. No-shows are charged the full amount.
